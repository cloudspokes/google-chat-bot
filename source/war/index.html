Send Message........Get Reminded!

My Reminder Bot is an Automated Google Talk Bot that will notify you of your reminders. Tell the Bot about any of your everyday tasks and when you would like to get notified (days, hours or in minutes).

At the time that the appointment is due, My Reminder Bot will dutifully notify you via a Google Talk Instant Message about your reminder.

Say Goodbye to all To Do applications and integrate reminders right within your popular Google Talk Chat. Refer to the 2 minute guide to off load your reminders today!
