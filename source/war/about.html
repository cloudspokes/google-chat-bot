About My Reminder Bot

My Reminder Bot is my submission to the Google Chat Bot Reminder contest in the CloudSpokes community.

CloudSpokes is Crowd Sourced community that brings together individuals working with various Cloud Computing technologies. Participate in challenges, build a diverse portfolio, learn something new, and earn a little cash money on the side too. The CloudSpokes community was launched by Appirio.
